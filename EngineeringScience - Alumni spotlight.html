The most significant strength of Engineering Science program at Pitt:

  • Flexibility schedules help students keep focus on their interest.
  • Analytic science components in the program prepare students exceptionally for the graduate school and industry.
  • Students will have a much broader view on natural sciences and build up a more intact structure of the theories and equations due to the interdisciplinary nature of the program.
